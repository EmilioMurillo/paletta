    PALETTA

    was formed in 2016 as a creative collective based in Boston, MA by Brian, Emilio, and Jesus.

    We were just kids who needed a creative outlet to produce projects we could be proud of.

    In 2022, PALETTA is now a design and innovation company.

    Q3 2016

    EVENT: DARK AND COLORFUL

    PROJECT: SPECTRUM

    FASHION: MIGLADI

    ART: ZVEZ

    PERFORMANCE: ZVEZ LIGHTSHOW

    SHOWCASE: HOUSE OF MIGLADI

    DESCRIPTION:

    We got the opportunity to make an event happen and so we made it happen. We were broke, had never made an event happen, but it was summer so why not. In “Dark and Colorful” we showcased two short films, ZVEZ art and a performed light show projected onto the art, Two performances by Josh and Monah, A fashion show for the pieces MIGLADI created.


    The event was mainly focused on the feeling of transmuting pain into something beautiful.
